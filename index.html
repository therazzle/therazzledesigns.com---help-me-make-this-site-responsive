About me

Hello and welcome to TheRazzleDesigns.com!

As long as I can remember, I have always been the creative type of person. From playing with lego, making music, to writing until my fingers fell off and I have had to tape them back together. That is what creation is for me.

I have made this site to reshare my expertise to others, to help them reach their goals. Even if the goal lies within your company, association, or band, perhaps in an event you organize, or whatever. Regardless, I’d like to help you!

I make custom logos, posters, business cards, cover photo to your Facebook page, and much more. The easiest way to find out what I have to offer, would be to contact me through the contact form near the bottom of this page. Or if you just have a question or two, feel free to contact me and I will respond as fast as I can!

Thanks for sticking out through this paragraph, and I hope to see you soon!

– Rasmus Eldblad | The Razzle Designs
